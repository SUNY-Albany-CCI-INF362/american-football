About Us

Empire Justice is a non-profit law firm focusing on achieving social and economic justice for the people in the New York State Area.

Empire Justice Center goal is to protect, strengthen and enlighten the people in the New York State area of their legal rights.

They are here to educate not just only homeowners but everyone. The goal is to make sure everyone is aware and understands the foreclosure procress. This should be an educating and a learning experience for all who comes to the website.
