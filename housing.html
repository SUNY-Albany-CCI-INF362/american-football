Find A Housing Counselor

We strongly advise you to obtain the FREE assistance of a local housing counselor. A housing counselor can assist you in communicating with your mortgagelender. Taking the first step of contacting a housing counseling agency and making an appointment will help you immensely in this foreclosure process.
