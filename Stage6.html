Stage 6: SETTLEMENT CONFERENCE

This is an opportunity for you to sit down with the Bank at your local courthouse to try to work out an alternative to foreclosure so that you may keep your home. You will find out about the time and date of the Settlement Conference in a letter sent to you by the Court.
