FILLING OUT THE NOTICE OF APPEARANCE

STEP 1: FILL OUT THE “CAPTION” OF THE NOTICE OF APPEARANCE

As shown in the sample Notice of Appearance above, fill in the top part of the Notice of Appearance form (known as the “Caption”) by copying the necessary information from the Summons or Complaint.

STEP 2: FILL OUT THE FIRST PAGE OF THE NOTICE OF APPEARANCE

Fill out the first page of the form as indicated below, including signature, current address, and your current and most reliable telephone number. Don’t forget to date the document!
